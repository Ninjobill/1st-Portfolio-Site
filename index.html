About Me

My whole life I've loved making and creating things with my own hands, with whatever I had around me and these creative ideas that always fill my head. This has brought the most joy to me. So when I discovered Web Development, it wasn't just a home run, it was a Grand Slam! I can literally create whatever I want, how I want it and tweak it till its just right. I have the worlds resources at my fingertips, answers whenever I need it and an empty canvas(Text Editor/IDE) to make all these ideas in my head real.

I love how all of the Web can be as responsive as it is. Web pages can fit 60" TV's to your Mobile phone and not lose any values and still look just as amazing. It's Incredibly satisfying. I like to think of myself like a website, Unique and Adaptable to all situations.

My Specialties include quickly learning new skills and Technology Stacks, Problem Solving, Responsive Design, Website Optimization and Model View Controller (MVC) method of organizing code.
